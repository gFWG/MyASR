Signal definition:
    config_changed = Signal(object)   # class-level attribute of SettingsDialog

Signal emission (in _on_save):
    new_config = self._collect_config()
    save_config(new_config)
    self.config_changed.emit(new_config)   # emits AppConfig instance
    logger.info("SettingsDialog: config saved and signal emitted")
    self.close()

Signal is emitted ONLY on Save, NOT on Cancel.
